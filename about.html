About KitchenFireExtinguisher

Who we are, how we review, and what our affiliate links mean.

What this site is for

KitchenFireExtinguisher.com exists to help people buy the right fire-safety gear for home kitchens without wading through generic listicles written by people who have clearly never thought about grease fires for more than twelve seconds.

We focus on practical home use: what belongs in a real kitchen, where to mount it, and what ordinary people are likely to use correctly under stress.

How we review

We evaluate products based on type rating, build quality, rechargeability, ease of placement, ease of use, and whether the recommendation actually makes sense for a home kitchen. We also weigh owner feedback and manufacturer reputation.

Affiliate disclosure

Full transparency: KitchenFireExtinguisher.com is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program that allows websites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.com.
  • If you click an Amazon link and buy something, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.
  • That does not change our rankings. We would rather be useful than squeeze out one more low-quality click.
  • Not every product we mention is linked, and not every good product makes the final list.

Editorial approach

We are biased toward useful safety advice over hype. That means you will see us recommend boring, proven equipment more often than flashy gadgets. Fire safety is not the place for gimmicks.
