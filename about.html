About Us

About KitchenFireExtinguisher.com

Who We Are

KitchenFireExtinguisher.com is an independent review site focused on kitchen fire safety products. We're part of the What Are Best review network — a collection of niche review sites that prioritize honest, data-driven product recommendations over paid placements and brand deals.

Our Methodology

We don't accept free products or paid placements. Our rankings are based on:

  • Amazon review data: We analyze thousands of verified reviews, focusing on products with 500+ reviews and 4.4+ star ratings
  • UL certification: Every extinguisher we recommend is UL listed and independently tested
  • Fire class ratings: We verify that products are rated for the fire types they claim to handle
  • Return rate analysis: Products with high return rates get flagged, regardless of star rating
  • Real-world usability: We consider weight, deployment speed, and ease of use under stress
